Tteokbokki (Spicy Korean Rice Cake)

This is the best recipe you will ever make!

Ingredients

  • Rice cake
  • Water
  • Hot pepper paste
  • Hot pepper flakes
  • Sugar
  • Green onions
  • Hard boiled eggs
  • fish cakes
  • cheese

Directions

  1. Boil Water
  2. Add rice Cake
  3. Combine hot pepper paste, hot pepper flakes, and sugar in a small bowl.
  4. Add the mixture in the bowl, the green onion, the fish cakes and hard boiled eggs.
  5. Stir gently for 10-15 minutes.
  6. Remove from the heat, put cheese on top and serve.
